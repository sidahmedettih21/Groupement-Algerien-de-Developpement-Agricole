Who We Are

Algeria's Agricultural Development Engine

GADA (Groupement Algérien de Développement Agricole) is a national GME headquartered in Relizane, operating across all 58 wilayas. We combine engineering expertise, digital analytics, laboratory science, and satellite technology to modernize Algeria's agricultural sector in full alignment with the Ministry of Agriculture's national strategy.

Our Mission
Transform Algeria's agricultural sector through technology, engineering, and intelligent data systems.
Our Vision
Position Algeria as a regional leader in smart, sustainable agriculture by 2030.
National Alignment
All operations aligned with Ministry of Agriculture strategic priorities and national digitization plans.
Food Security
Contributing directly to Algeria's food independence through optimized production and early warning systems.

Key Capabilities

What the Platform Does

01
Real-Time Animal Health Monitoring
Temperature, movement, and herd separation tracked live across all farms. Automatic alerts sent to farm managers via SMS and WhatsApp before any issue escalates.
02
72-Hour Early Warning System
The system analyzes behavioral and health data to detect early signs of illness up to 72 hours before visible symptoms — enabling intervention before losses occur.
03
Financial Monitoring Across All Branches
Every transaction across every branch is monitored in real time. Unusual patterns are flagged automatically to protect the organization's financial integrity.
04
Smart Irrigation & Water Management
Soil sensors combined with weather data and crop type calculate the precise daily water requirement per field. Automated irrigation control reduces consumption by up to 40%.
05
National Production Forecasting
The platform predicts national agricultural output by crop type 3 to 6 months ahead — enabling the Ministry to plan imports and exports and prevent food shortages before they occur.
06
Product Traceability & Export Certification
Every animal receives a verified digital record from birth. This enables automatic halal certification and compliance with European and Gulf export standards.
07
Direct Farm-to-Market Platform
A digital marketplace connecting farms directly with restaurants, hotels, and exporters — removing intermediaries so farmers earn more and buyers access better quality.
08
Automated Reports for Leadership
Weekly and monthly performance reports generated and sent automatically — covering livestock health, financial results, and operational summaries per branch and wilaya.

Strategic Vision

Our Roadmap
to 2030

GADA is built in four clear phases — from a solid operational foundation across Algeria, to becoming the country's leading agricultural technology company.

Our Commitment
Every system we build is local, secure, and owned by Algeria. Our data stays in Algeria, our technology is maintained in Algeria, and our expertise grows within Algeria — building long-term national capability.
